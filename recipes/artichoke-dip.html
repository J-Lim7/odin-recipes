Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

Description

A hot, delicious, and cheesy spinach artichoke dip that is the perfect way to get your friends and family to start enjoying the party.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese: 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • Mayonnaise: 1/4 cup
  • Cheeses: grated Parmesan, grated Romano, and shredded mozzarella: 1/4 cup each
  • Garlic: 1 clove, peeled and minced
  • Spices and seasonings: dried basil, garlic salt, salt, and pepper: 1/2 teaspoon dried basil and garlic salt; salt and pepper to your taste preference
  • Artichoke hearts: 1 (14 ounce) can, drained and chopped
  • Spinach: 1/2 cup frozen and chopped, thawed and drained

How to make the Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip

  1. Mix all the ingredients (except for the mozzarella) together.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a prepared baking dish. Top with cheese.
  3. Bake the spinach artichoke dip in the preheated oven until bubbly.
